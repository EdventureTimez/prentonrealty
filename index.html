The Opportunity

Why Florida & Texas

The same fundamentals that drive our international thesis — population growth, capital inflows, lifestyle demand — are reshaping these domestic markets.

Tax Advantage

Both Florida and Texas offer zero state income tax, driving a sustained migration of high-net-worth individuals and businesses from higher-tax states.

Population Growth

Florida and Texas are the #1 and #2 states for net domestic migration. More people means more demand for housing, commercial space, and services.

Corporate Relocations

Major companies continue moving headquarters and operations to these states. Where companies go, executive housing and commercial real estate follow.
